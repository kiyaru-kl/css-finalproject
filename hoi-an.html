Hoi An: The Ancient Lantern Town

Hoi An Ancient Town is a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its well-preserved architecture, custom tailoring, and enchanting lantern-lit nights. It offers a tranquil contrast to the bustling cities, perfect for those seeking charm and history.

Key Attractions

Travel Tips for Hoi An

  • Rent a bicycle to explore the town and surrounding rice paddies.
  • Get custom clothes made by local tailors.
  • Attend a lantern festival if your visit coincides.
  • Try the local specialties like Cao Lau and White Rose dumplings.

Don't miss the wonders of Ho Chi Minh City or Ha Long Bay!
