Ho Chi Minh City: The Dynamic Metropolis

Formerly known as Saigon, Ho Chi Minh City is the largest city in Vietnam and a vibrant hub of culture, commerce, and history. From its French colonial architecture to its bustling markets and delicious street food, HCMC offers an exhilarating experience.

Key Attractions

Travel Tips for HCMC

  • Try the local street food, especially Banh Mi and Pho.
  • Bargain politely at markets.
  • Be aware of motorbike traffic.
  • Visit during the dry season (December to April) for best weather.
