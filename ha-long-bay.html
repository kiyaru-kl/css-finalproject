Ha Long Bay: A Natural Wonder

Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is famous for its emerald waters and thousands of towering limestone islands topped with rainforests. Cruising through this surreal landscape is an unforgettable experience, offering stunning vistas and unique activities.

Key Activities & Sights

Travel Tips for Ha Long Bay

  • Book your cruise in advance, especially during peak season.
  • Consider an overnight cruise for the full experience.
  • Pack sunscreen, a hat, and swimwear.
  • Look for tours that include kayaking and cave visits.
